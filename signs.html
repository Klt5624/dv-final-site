Warning signs

Abuse often escalates. Recognizing early signs can help someone seek support sooner. These lists are for education — every situation is unique.

Control & isolation

  • Jealousy framed as “love”
  • Monitoring phone, location, or social media
  • Limiting contact with friends/family
  • Demanding passwords or access

Intimidation & threats

  • Breaking objects, punching walls
  • Threats about children, pets, or money
  • Threatening self-harm to control you
  • Showing weapons or “joking” about harm

Manipulation

  1. Blaming you for their behavior
  2. Gaslighting (“that never happened”)
  3. Love-bombing after harm
  4. Public charm, private cruelty

Red-flag behaviors (hover effects)

Hover over the cards to see CSS transforms and emphasis.

Financial control

Taking paychecks, blocking access to accounts, forcing debt, or controlling basic necessities.

Digital abuse

Tracking apps, constant texts, demanding immediate replies, or using social media to humiliate.

Coercion

Pressuring sex, sabotaging birth control, or using threats/“punishment” to get compliance.
